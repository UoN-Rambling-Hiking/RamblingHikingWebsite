Subscribing To Calendar

  • Socials
    https://ramsoc.co.uk/socials/index.ics
  • Walks
    https://ramsoc.co.uk/walks/index.ics
  • Socials & Walks
    https://calendar.ramsoc.co.uk
  1. Login to your calendar.
  2. Add URL to calendar.
    • Google Calander: Click the other calendars down arrow. Add from URL.
    • Office 365/Outlook: Click the add calendar button. Select from internet
  3. Copy a URL from above, ensuring it is exact.
    • Do not download the calendar as it will not update automatically
  4. Save the calendar subscription.
  5. Calendar will now be displayed in your chosen applicaiton.

If you're struggling to add the calendar to your calendar app, ask committee at your next event
